Cookies

What are cookies?

This website uses cookies. Cookies are small files that are being sent with pages of this website that your browser saves to the hard-disk of your computer. The information in the cookies can be sent back to our servers during your next visit. 

Use of permanent cookies 

By use of permanent cookies we can recognize you when you visit this website again. In this way the website can be configured to your preferences. In this way you never have to configure your preferences so you save time for a more enjoyable visit to our website. Permanent cookies can be deleted in the settings of your browser.
